### 🎓 The Teaching Philosophy

I don't gatekeep. I don't lecture from a pedestal. I sit next to my students and we figure things out together. The best moment in teaching is when someone says *"I actually get it now"* — and you can see the exact second it clicks.

My classrooms are inclusive, chaotic in the best way, and built on the idea that **anyone** can learn this stuff if the right person explains it the right way. I've had biologists, designers, people with zero tech background walk through my courses and come out the other side as working professionals.
